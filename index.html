About us

The weather can change in a split second. Your safety depends on timely nowcasts and forecasts that provide understandable information you can use. This website presents weather observations, weather forecasts and climatological information for selected cities/countries. Weather icons are shown alongside worded forecasts in this version to facilitate visual inspection.
